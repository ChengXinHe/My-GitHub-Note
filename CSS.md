* **Universal Selector**
  >` *{`

* **Element, Id, Class selector**
  >id唯一 但一个可以有很多class
  >element:
  `p.center {text-align:center;}`
  >id:
    ```#para1```
    ```{text-align:center;```
    ```color:red;}```
  >Class:
    `.center {text-align:center;}`

* **Grouping Selector**
  >h1,h2{}
* **descendant selector**
  >div.class a{}
